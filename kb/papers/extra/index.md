# Extra & Emerging

## Scope

This section collects papers that cross thematic boundaries or represent fields not yet consolidated enough for a dedicated section. Current topics include ferroelectric synaptic devices, organic electrochemical transistors, hybrid SNN-ANN architectures, advanced AER communication systems, stochastic neuromorphic computing, and in-sensor neuromorphic vision.

## Relevance

The frontier of neuromorphic research appears here before it stabilises into a subfield. Ferroelectric and organic devices offer properties oxide RRAM cannot: nanosecond polarisation switching with CMOS compatibility in the former, soft-matter biocompatibility and ion-mediated plasticity in the latter. Hybrid SNN-ANN architectures acknowledge that spiking and rate-coded computation are each optimal for different tasks, and that deployed systems will blend both.
